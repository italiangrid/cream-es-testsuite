TEST CASE: Delegation
Full Name:Cream Es Test Suite.Delegation
Documentation:Create a delegation and check that its lifetime is the same as the proxy it originated from. A tolerance of 5 minutes (300 seconds) is used, just in case the clocks of the hosts aren't synchronized.
Nodes must be syncronized for this test to work.
